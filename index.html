Chefs

Chef Jacques Lemerre spent two years at Paley’s Place before moving to New York City to begin his formal training at the French Culinary Institute. Brian’s love of butchery led him to Brooklyn’s renowned Heritage Foods USA. There he worked directly with farmers, breeders and butchers devoted to raising and producing heritage breeds of pork, beef and lamb

Chef Melanie Cousteau grew up in Salem, Oregon and attended Grinell College where she majored in Chinese and Anthropology. While attending school, she started working in restaurants and soon found it to be more fun than what she was studying at the time. It was during her fellowship in France, that she began working in a kitchen and bar for fun and knew she wanted to try it out. She came back to the Pacific Northwest, and attended culinary school at Le Cordon Bleu.

About

At La Fenetre we embrace the french cuisine with a northwest flare. La fenetre means window and in our minds food can be that window opening you up to another world. Chef Jacques Lemerre and Melanie Cousteau create weekly menus that reflect their love for the seasonal flavors of Oregon as well as the french countryside. The freshest local produce,organic, pasture-raised meats from our favorite farms are thoughtfully paired with outstanding wines and delicate cocktails to please and delight the senses. A meal at La Fenetre is an intimate experience.Our wine and cocktail lists take their cues from the kitchen and are tastefully selected by wine director Lara Lee Mosley. We look forward to serving you soon.
